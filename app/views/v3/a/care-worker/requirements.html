Duty of care

You have a duty of care towards individuals receiving care and support in your workplace and to other workers.

This means promoting well-being and making sure people are kept safe from harm, abuse and injury.

As part of your duty of care, you should pass on any concerns about well-being or safety.

It’s a legal requirement, and you can’t choose whether to do it.

Duty of care is embedded in the codes of conduct and professional practice.
