Duty of care. This standard looks at the term "duty of care", and how this relates to your role when providing care and support to individuals and when working with your team and other professionals. Sometimes there may be difficult situations in your work. This standard will help you to understand processes to follow and approaches to implement to maintain a duty of care. This standard is broken down into five topics. Understanding how duty of care contributes to safe practice, Understanding the support which is available for addressing dilemmas that may arise about duty of care, dealing with comments and complaints, dealing with incidents, errors and near misses, dealing with confrontation and difficult situations. This standard includes what you need to know and be able to do in your role. Your employer will explain how you'll be able to access and complete the required learning for this standard and how and when you will be assessed.
