Information for employers

  • The care workforce pathway

    The career pathways for adult social care are designed to help people understand their career options in adult social care. It also helps them understand where they are in their career. It contains information about the different job role categories in adult social care, starting with 4 direct care roles: entry-level, care or support worker, supervisor or leader, and practice leader.

  • Skills Record

    The Skills Record keeps all your training and practical experience in one place.
