The care workforce pathway

The care workforce pathway sets out clearly what a career in social care means and the level of knowledge, experience and skills required to deliver high-quality, personalised, care and support.

It will empower people working in social care to develop themselves and their career by setting out how they can gain skills, access learning and development, and progress in their careers in a way that meets their aspirations.

Next steps for the pathway

We are developing further role categories for:

  • Deputy Manager
  • Registered Manager
  • Enhanced/Complex care
  • Personal Assistant

We will be engaging further with the sector for insight and feedback on what knowledge, skills and behaviours each of these require.

How to adopt the care workforce pathway

The pathway is designed to set the direction of learning and development across the adult social care sector going forward. We recommend that any employer in the sector should consider the pathway and think about how they can apply it.

We will continue to add resources to the pathway to help people across the sector use it widely. This includes a new digital product that will link through to training opportunities.

How to use the pathway

Use the new adult social care pathway values

The values were a result of sector collaboration and co-design with representatives from the sector. Everyone in adult social care should have these values and be working towards them. These may complement any existing organisation values you have.

Map onto the pathway job roles

Look at how your current organisational structures and job roles map onto the pathway. The breakdown of each job category may be helpful for workers to understand their job role and how they can develop or train in their role, or progress to a related role if that is what they want to do.

Guide career conversations

The pathway is in the public domain and available to access and use by anyone interested in it. Workers in adult social care can take it away and read it or you can show and discuss it with them.
