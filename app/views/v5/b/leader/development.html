Areas of specialism

For specialisms related to practice leaders, the Department of Health and Social Care (DHSC) is looking at how suggested learning opportunities meet the needs of the sector, and are of good quality and value.

DHSC is still identifying appropriate learning opportunities for practice leaders.

{% set specialismsHtml %}

These are some of the options available for practice leaders to do professional development. This will be updated soon.

Specific areas of practice and suggested learning opportunities or resources

  • autism: Core Capabilities Framework for Supporting Autistic People
  • dementia: Dementia Training Standards Framework
  • end of life care: End of Life Care Core Skills Education and Training Framework
  • learning disabilities: Core Capabilities Framework for Supporting People with a Learning Disability
  • mental health: Mental Health Core Skills Education and Training Framework
  • trauma-informed care and relational working
  • drugs and alcohol
  • sensory impairments (and communication)
  • digital skills: Adult Social Care Digital Skills Framework
